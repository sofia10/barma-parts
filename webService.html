Web Service

Barma Web Service - Automated Prices & Availability

Barma Web Service - a tool that allows the possibility of your site or application to automatically interact with us in real time.

You can:

  • receive information on the price and quantity available;
  • receive information about the requested product analogs;
  • to place orders with us;
  • monitor the status of the previously placed orders.

Advantages of Barma Web Service

If you are our client and you already have a website or are you are just going to develop a website, our Web Service will give you a competitive advantage.

1. Automatic reflection of price and balances.

You and your customers will always have the latest information on our product. This will reduce the number of failures due to unreliable data. For example, if the parts have been sold or the price of it has changed.

2. Automatic backup product.

You will be able to create reserves on our website automatically, without human intervention. It will save your employees' time for other tasks.

3. Automatic tracking statuses in reserve.

You will be able to automatically track the status of your order. This allows you to inform your client about the stages of delivery.

4. Flexible configuration.

Our Web Service supports all the basic functions for creating and processing of applications.

Get Started with Barma Web Service in 3 simple steps:

Step 1

Finalize your contract with one of our managers.

Step 2

Register a new user in the system (if you are existing customer you can use the same user name and password).

Step 3

Download, install, configure, and begin using our web service benefits, it is necessary to resort to the help of a technician (programmer). Barma Auto Parts LLC are not involved in setting up the web service and accompanying software.
