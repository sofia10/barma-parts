How to become a client?

For registration of the first order, you need to sign up and register in the system or contact the company's representative at your region.
5 Steps to start cooperation

1. SIGN UP

  • Click on “SIGN UP” button and complete the registration process by filling the requested information in the required filed.
  • Below listed is the screen print of the registration window. All the fields marked with bold TITLES has to be filled in.
  • Once you click on Submit button, the following text will appear:
Your Account has been successfully created. You can use Your account after receiving a confirmation email.
  • Also, you will receive a confirmation letter from Barma Auto Spare Parts stating that, you are now able to use the services of our website.

2. SEARCH

  • In order to find the prices of auto spare parts in our website You have to type the part number in the window of «search by part number» and click on «Search» button
  • Two subtitles will appear as a result of search. Under the subtitle «The requested number» appears the part number that you preliminary search for.
  • Under the subtitle «Original Replacement, analogue» appears the list of replacement brands corresponding to your requested part number.
  • In the column «Make» - The Manufacturing brand name will appear.
  • In the column «Part number» - The spare part number will appear.
  • In the column «Description» - The detailed name of the spare part will appear.
  • In the column «In Stock» - The stock information of suppliers as well as the availability chart and the delivery statistics will be displayed.
  • In the column «Weight, kg» – The weight of the spare part in kilograms will be displayed.
  • In the column «Price» - The price of the spare part in US Dollars will be displayed.
  • In the column «Location» – The number of each supplier that refers only to us will be displayed.
  • In the column «Delivery Days» – The number of days it will take to deliver the spare parts to our warehouse will be displayed.
  • On the right corner of the page you will see the "cart" button and while pressing the cart the following information will appear:
  • Choose the quantity you want to order
  • In «Reference» option you may write any information that you consider important otherwise leave it empty
  • Click on «Add» button.

3. ORDER

To place order you have to do the following:

  • Enter the page «Cart»
  • Select «Delivery type»
  • Select «Delivery location»
  • Select «Excess percent».
  • Select «Place Order» to process the order of the selected items from the cart.

4. PAYMENT

  • You may find our company Bank Transfer Details by visiting «Finance» - «Payment».

5. ORDER STATUS

After processing the order from the cart the system of the web site creates a number to your order. That order you may see by visiting «Order» – «Order Status» in the Menu.

The Status meanings are:

  • «Registered» - means the ordered parts has been accepted by our system and you may cancel them if any changes happen.
  • «In Process» – The order has been sent to Supplier. Here you will not be able to cancel the order.
  • «Purchased» – We have received a confirmation from the supplier about the delivery of the order.
  • «Delivered» – We have already received the order to our warehouse and our staff is going sort and repack it.
  • «Not Available» – The ordered spare part is not available with the supplier.
  • While placing order if you have selected the option «N» it means that you want to receive only the specified part number and no superseded part number will be delivered to you.
  • «Canceled» – The Order has been cancelled by you.
  • «Price Changed» – The price of the spare part has been changed. Here you will have an option either to cancel or confirm the order with the new price. You will receive a notification email as well. So Please check your email regularly.
  • «Approved by client» – You have already confirmed the new price of the spare part.
  • «Sent to Client» – The ordered spare part has been already delivered to the warehouse of Cargo Company.
  • «Reordered» – The spare part has been reordered to another supplier.
  • «Cancelled by Manager» – The Manager has cancelled the order.

For QUOTE THROUGH EXCEL

  • Go to «Menu»
  • Select «Cart»
  • Select «Quote»
  • Press the button «Upload Excel» and select the required excel file from your computer and the quotation results will appear on your screen. The format of the Excel file must be «Excel Workbook 1997-2003 xls».
  • Inside Excel file the first column must contain part number, the second column must contain the quantity and the third column must contain Reference (not obligatory, you may leave it empty).
  • Press the button «Download Excel» if you want the quotation results to appear in an Excel sheet.
  • Press button «Add to Cart» to send the quotation result to cart.
  • Press button «Clear» to delete the quotation results.
  • For those part numbers where you do not get any quotation, you may have to send it to Manager to get prices by clicking on the «Send Inquiry» button.
  • To see the response from the Manager to the sent query you may visit «Cart» – «Inquiry».
  • We are always open to suggestions and will be grateful for any feedback on info@barmaparts.com
