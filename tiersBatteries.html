Tiers & Batteries

Tiers

Barma Auto Parts LLC offers a wide range of quality tires for a variety of applications. Our offering lines include:

Nankang, Westlake, Regal Altura, Amine,TVS and Duratough tyres.

To buy or find out the price and availability of any tyre:

  1. Type in the search box Tyre Size in the following order: The tyre section width in millimetres X The aspect ratio in % (the height of the sidewall divided by the tyre's width) X Denotes the tyre's construction type X Rim diameter in inches eg. 185x60xR14
  2. Select the desired sizes & quantities, add it to your cart and place your order. For further ordering follow the instructions that appear on the screen.
