Suppliers

How to become a supplier?

In order to become our supplier, you must:

  1. Send the price list by e-mail to new-supplier@barmaparts.com
  2. Provide information about your company (see below -. requirements).

Requirements to suppliers

  1. A variety of product range:
    • original spare parts - no less than 5,000 articles;
    • aftermarket auto parts - not less than 7000 articles (with the presence of technical description);
    • for manufacturers representative - not less than 4000 articles.
  2. he certificates for the products offered and providing guarantees for a period of not less than 6 months.
  3. A flexible system of discounts.
  4. The ability to return the goods. he range and current balances in warehouses.

The work flow with supplier

  • Close supervision to execute orders on time;
  • Daily non-stop reception of the parts, without interruption and days off;
  • No counterfeit parts;
  • Payment to be made by bank transfer.

For any questions please contact info@barmaparts.com.

Required documents

Before signing the contract, a new supplier shall provide copies of the following documents, certified by the company seal the original and signed by the director:

  1. Valid Trade License
  2. Credentials of the head of the legal person (the person signing the contract);
  3. Document confirming the legal address of the company
  4. A copy of a power of attorney for the right of employees to sign primary shipping documents and invoices for the director and chief accountant;

To discuss any supply opportunity, please contact us via e-mail: new-supplier@barmaparts.com
